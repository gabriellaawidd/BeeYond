Learning Outcome

LO1: Thoroughly define the fundamental laws of calculus including limits, derivatives, and integrals.
LO2: Comprehensively explain the properties of derivatives and integrals and their applications.
LO3: Comprehensively explain the properties of integrals and their applications in solving real-world problems.
LO4: Systematically compute the derivatives of various functions using differentiation rules.

About This Course

The course emphasizes on understanding of basic concepts of calculus including limits, derivatives, integral and infinite series. These basic concepts would be interpreted geometrically and physically, and applied to solving various problems. The calculus and its applications are fundamental to many areas of mathematics, science, and engineering. This course provides a thorough introduction to differential and integral calculus. Students will learn to compute derivatives and integrals, and to apply these concepts to solve problems involving rates of change, optimization, and area under curves. The course will also cover techniques for evaluating limits, finding derivatives using various rules, and computing integrals using substitution and various integration or integration by parts methods. At the end, a knowledge of infinite series and their convergence will be introduced. Upon completion of this course, students will have a solid foundation in calculus that will prepare them for advanced mathematics courses, science courses, and engineering courses.
